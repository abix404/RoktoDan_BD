Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a seamless, technology-driven ecosystem that connects blood donors with recipients instantly, ensuring no patient faces a shortage of blood in critical moments. We strive to build a community of compassionate donors committed to saving lives.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's most trusted blood donation network, where every person in need can find a donor within minutes. We envision a future where blood shortage is a thing of the past, and communities unite to support each other through the gift of life.

Our Story

How It All Began

RoktoDan BD was born from a personal experience of struggle in finding blood donors during a critical emergency. We witnessed firsthand how precious time is wasted searching for donors through phone calls and social media posts.

This inspired us to create a platform that leverages technology to solve this life-threatening problem. Today, we connect thousands of donors with recipients, ensuring that help is just a few clicks away.

Our journey is driven by the belief that no one should lose a loved one due to blood unavailability when there are willing donors in the community.
